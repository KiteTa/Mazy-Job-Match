# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A solo job seeker (new-grad SWE) hunting for US roles that offer visa sponsorship. Uses this daily, alone, on a laptop. The tool is personal infrastructure — not shared, not public.

## Product Purpose

A self-hosted daily job discovery dashboard. Scrapes Greenhouse and Ashby listings, filters for relevance (location, visa, seniority), and surfaces the best matches in a fast, readable UI. Success = fewer irrelevant listings, faster triage, more confident applications.

## Brand Personality

Clean, warm, personal. Feels like a well-crafted personal tool — not a product shipped to thousands. Quiet confidence, no noise.

## Anti-references

- LinkedIn, Indeed: corporate blue, cluttered, recruiter-spam energy, dense aggressive typography
- Hard professional "business software" aesthetic: rigid grid cards, small gray text, ugly table layouts
- Generic SaaS dashboards: metric hero cards, gradient accents, cream/sand AI-default palette

## Design Principles

1. **Personal over generic** — feels handmade for one person, not designed for a market segment
2. **Calm density** — show a lot of information without feeling overwhelming; whitespace is intentional
3. **Warmth without softness** — the palette is warm but the interactions are crisp and fast
4. **Text first** — job listings are text; typography and readability take priority over chrome
5. **No noise** — every element earns its place; decoration that doesn't inform gets cut

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigation already supported (j/k arrow keys). Reduced motion respected.
